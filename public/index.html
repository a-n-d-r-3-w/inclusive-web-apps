Inclusive Web Apps

This website serves inclusive apps that support individual health and growth. Inclusive web apps are:

  1. Accessible: They conform to Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 so that they are more accessible to people with disabilities.
  2. Low cost to use: The apps are currently free to use and we welcome donations to help offset operating costs. If operating costs become unsustainable, we will adjust this approach.
  3. Performant on slow network connections: Not everybody has a fiber-optic gigabit internet connection. Users might live or be traveling in an area where a high-speed connection is not available or not affordable. Inclusive Web Apps are written to be performant for users with limited bandwidth. There are no ads, trackers, or analytics that waste bandwidth.
  4. Legible and usable on small screens

Why does the website look so plain?

Resources are limited. Not only web app-development resources, but also your network bandwidth and device battery life. We choose to focus our resources on inclusive web design and implementation. We could spend time and money instead on making the website look fancy with rounded buttons, subtle colors and animations, but this is more code that consumes your device's network bandwidth and battery life, and sometimes such features hinder accessibility. There are enough people out there who prioritize aesthetics and flair. We need more people to prioritize inclusiveness.
